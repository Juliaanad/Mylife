Elegance
🥪 Sandwich Method
Match top color = shoe color. Creates one visual line making legs look endless. Best for 159cm.
💚 Monochrome Method
Head-to-toe one color. Instantly looks expensive and makes you look taller.
↕ Vertical Line Method
Pinstripes, V-necks, open cardigans, long necklaces. Draws eye up = longer torso.
🎨 Tonal Dressing
Different shades of same color. Cream + beige + tan. Very quiet luxury.
⚖ Proportion Method
Fitted top + wide bottom OR volume top + slim bottom. Never both baggy.
👚 Tucking Method
Half-tuck into high-waist bottoms always. Creates waist, lengthens torso.
🎨 60-30-10 Color Rule
60% dominant · 30% secondary · 10% accent. Never more than 3 colors.
👖
High Waist Wide Leg Pants
Your #1 bottom. Adds hip curves + lengthens torso.
